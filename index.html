Rodger Benham

PhD Candidate at RMIT University
E-mail: rodger.benham / rmit.edu.au

My work aims to extend risk-sensitive retrieval approaches, to improve the efficiency and effectiveness of large-scale search engines.

I previously worked in the software industry at Codeacious, Vivi Australia, and Friday Media (now ASTA).

For an up-to-date curriculum vitae, please visit my LinkedIn page. Some travel photos can be found here.

Research Interests

Efficient and Effective Information Retrieval, IR Evaluation, Query Refinement, and Statistical Inference.
