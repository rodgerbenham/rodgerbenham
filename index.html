About

I am a PhD Candidate in Computer Science at RMIT as of March 2018, under the supervision of Associate Professor J. Shane Culpepper (RMIT) and Professor Alistair Moffat (University of Melbourne). My work will aim to improve the efficiency and effectiveness of search engines for a variety of different use cases.

I have industry experience in full-stack cloud-based web development, desktop application, mobile and embedded device development and building virtual reality experiences. For an up-to-date curriculum vitae, please visit my LinkedIn page.

Research Interests

High-Recall Retrieval, Evaluation, Text Indexing and Compression, Efficiency, Query Refinement.
